Core Responsibilities

TCC manages both Crew Transport (CTM) and Chauffeur Drive Services (CDS), including:

  • Geo-coding
  • Vehicle tracking and monitoring
  • Manual and automated job allocation
  • Passenger (PAX) services for Cabin Crew, Pilots, VIPs

Transport Services Provided

  • Crew Transport (Pilots, Cabin Crew)
  • Chauffeur Drive Service (First, Business Class)
  • Airport transfers for ABC Engineers (e.g., to/from DWC)
  • Arabian Adventures & Other VIP Movements
  • Transfer Locations: Primarily at DXB, with extended coverage across UAE.

Echo Learning Guide (Beginners)

  • Tracking Tab: Vehicle details (plate #, driver, service provider, territory)
  • Driver Shift Tab: Weekly shift allocation and work hours
  • Bookings Tab: Data from NCC, MARS, and manual bookings
  • Geo-coding: Assign accurate locations for pickups and drops; resolve unassigned or "on hold" jobs
  • Extras to Validate: Child seats, booster seats, E-gate access, WCHC (wheelchair), etc.

Summary

The TCC operates as the nerve center for crew and premium guest transport logistics at Emirates. The Echo system is the main operational tool for real-time control, planning, and coordination, integrating data from internal systems and service providers to deliver smooth ground transport experiences.
