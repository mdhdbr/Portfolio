Safety & Inspection Training Portal

Standardized inspection practices to reduce operational risks and improve fleet uptime

Purpose

To standardize inspection practices, reduce operational risks, improve fleet uptime, and ensure inspections meet the industry safety standards.

Executive Briefing

  • Objective: Build a scalable inspection training system aligned with HSE and operational continuity.
  • Scope: Covers inspections, safety, digital tools, and fault reporting.
  • Outcome: Fewer breakdowns, higher uptime, better compliance, and trained staff.

Training Modules

Module Topic When Where How Who Output

5 Emergency Procedures Day 3 Control Room Scenario Drill Ops Team Readiness
6 Digital Reporting Day 4 IT Room Tablet Demo IT Support Report Accuracy
7 Final Review Day 5 Training Room Test & Task Committee Certification

Execution Flow

  • Before Training: Select participants, prepare LMS and checklists, notify schedule
  • During: 60% field, 40% classroom, daily briefings, feedback sessions
  • Post: Issue certificate, track performance, refresher every 6 months

Key Materials

  • Checklist Templates (Daily/Weekly)
  • Reference Photos & Fault Chart
  • Emergency Contacts Sheet
  • App-based Logging Guide
  • Supervisor Audit Sheet
