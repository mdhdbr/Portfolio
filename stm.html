ABC-STM Daily Operations Summary | Staff Transport Coordination

Overview

This document outlines the core tasks, operational coverage, physical and virtual responsibilities, and key metrics for Emirates (ABC) Staff Transport Management (STM), ensuring efficient movement of staff and compliance with safety standards.

Operational Objectives

  • Implement real-time learnings from physical operations to optimize virtual systems.
  • Adhere to ABC's Transport Policy, Safety Policy, and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).
  • Enhance customer satisfaction, safety, and operational efficiency.

Physical Tasks

  • Hourly Service Monitoring: Supervise inbound/outbound services and bus compliance checks.
  • Bus & Stop Audits: Ensure bus quality, driver grooming, and stop-point safety.
  • Surveys & Route Validation: Identify inactive pickup points, verify route timings, and address road closures.
  • Bus Checks: Conduct daily inspections using iAuditor; validate stop points not shown on maps.
  • Staff Eligibility Checks: On-board screening for authorized passengers.

Virtual Tasks

  • Email & Request Handling: Address user requests, respond to feedback, and issue rerouting based on demand.
  • Data Management: Update SharePoint, GroupWorld, and dashboards with operational metrics.
  • CRF Management: Process changes in routes and schedules upon admin requests.
  • Analysis & Reporting: Evaluate provider reports, track KPIs, duty hours, trip delays, and Salik usage.
  • Compliance Tracking: Ensure route, safety, and operational standards are maintained and communicated.
